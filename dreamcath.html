The Spirit of Notre Dame Cathedral

The Notre-Dame Cathedral is a stunning example of Gothic architecture located in Paris, France. I've always wanted to visit to admire its intricate sculptures, breathtaking stained glass windows, and the famous bell towers. Experiencing the history and standing where so many historical events took place would be incredible. Despite the tragic fire in 2019, the efforts to restore its beauty make it even more meaningful to see.

Here are some of the sights I'd love to see:
1. The rose windows with their vibrant stained glass designs.
2. The Gargoyles that guard the cathedral's exterior.
3. The view from the bell towers overlooking Paris.
